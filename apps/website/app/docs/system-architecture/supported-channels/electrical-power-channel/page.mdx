# Electrical Power Channel

<Callout type="info">
	**Channel Category**: `electrical_power`
</Callout>

Reports real-time power metrics such as voltage, current, power, and frequency for monitoring and diagnostics.

---

## Required Properties

| **Property** | **Data Type** | **Range/Values** | **Unit** | **Permissions** |
|--------------|---------------|------------------|----------|-----------------|
| `power` | Float | 0–10000 | W | RO (Read-Only) |

---

### Property Details

#### `power`

Power consumption in watts

| **Property** | **Data Type** | **Range/Values** | **Unit** | **Permissions** |
