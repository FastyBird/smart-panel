# Flow Channel

<Callout type="info">
	**Channel Category**: `flow`
</Callout>

Monitors the rate of fluid or air flow, typically measured in cubic meters per hour (m3/h).

---

## Required Properties

| **Property** | **Data Type** | **Range/Values** | **Unit** | **Permissions** |
|--------------|---------------|------------------|----------|-----------------|
| `rate` | Float | 0–65533 | m³/h | RO (Read-Only) |
