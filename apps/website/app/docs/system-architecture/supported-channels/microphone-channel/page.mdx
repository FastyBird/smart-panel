# Microphone Channel

<Callout type="info">
	**Channel Category**: `microphone`
</Callout>

Represents audio input devices, reporting activity or capturing sound.

---

## Required Properties

| **Property** | **Data Type** | **Range**    | **Unit** | **Permissions** |
|--------------|---------------|--------------|----------|-----------------|
| `active`     | `Bool`        | `true/false` | -        | RW (Read/Write) |

---

### Property Details

#### `active`

Indicates whether the microphone is muted:

- `true`: Audio input is active.
- `false`: Audio input is muted.

---

## Optional Properties

| **Property** | **Data Type** | **Range** | **Unit** | **Permissions** |
|--------------|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| `volume`     | `Uchar` or `Enum` | `0–100` or `mute/low/medium/high/max` | %        | RO (Read-Only)  |

---

### Property Details

#### `volume`

Represents the current volume/gain level. This property supports multiple data types:

**Option 1 - Percentage:**
- **Data Type**: `Uchar`
- **Range**: `0–100%`
- `0`: Minimum volume (silent).
- `100`: Maximum volume (full audio input).

**Option 2 - Discrete Levels:**
- **Data Type**: `Enum`
- **Values**: `mute`, `low`, `medium`, `high`, `max`
- Predefined volume levels.
