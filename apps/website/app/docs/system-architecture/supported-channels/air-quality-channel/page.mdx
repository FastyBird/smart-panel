# Air Quality Channel

<Callout type="info">
	**Channel Category**: `air_quality`
</Callout>

Reports overall air quality index (AQI) and quality level.

<Callout type="warning">
	At least one of `aqi` or `level` must be provided.
</Callout>

---

## Required Properties

At least one of the following properties must be present:

| **Property** | **Data Type** | **Range**                              | **Unit** | **Permissions** |
|--------------|---------------|----------------------------------------|----------|-----------------|
| `aqi`        | `Ushort`      | `0–500`                                | -        | RO (Read-Only)  |
| `level`      | `Enum`        | `excellent/good/fair/inferior/poor`    | -        | RO (Read-Only)  |

---

### Property Details

#### `aqi`

Air Quality Index (AQI) value:

- **Range**: `0–500`
- Lower values indicate better air quality.
- Standard AQI ranges:
  - `0–50`: Excellent
  - `51–100`: Good
  - `101–150`: Fair
  - `151–200`: Inferior
  - `201–500`: Poor

#### `level`

Air quality level classification:

- `excellent`: Air quality is excellent with minimal pollution.
- `good`: Air quality is good and acceptable.
- `fair`: Air quality is moderate; sensitive individuals may experience issues.
- `inferior`: Air quality is unhealthy for sensitive groups.
- `poor`: Air quality is unhealthy for all individuals.

---

## Optional Properties

| **Property** | **Data Type** | **Range**     | **Unit** | **Permissions** |
|--------------|---------------|---------------|----------|-----------------|
| `active`     | `Bool`        | `true/false`  | -        | RO (Read-Only)  |
| `fault`      | `Uchar`       | `0–255`       | -        | RO (Read-Only)  |

---

### Property Details

#### `active`

Indicates the channel's current working status:

- `true`: The sensor is active and functioning correctly.
- `false`: The sensor is inactive or non-functional.

#### `fault`

Represents the fault state of the sensor:

- `0`: No fault detected.
- Non-zero: A fault has been detected, potentially affecting functionality.
