# Streaming Service Device

<Callout type="info">
	**Device Category**: `streaming_service`
</Callout>

The Streaming Service device represents a cloud-based media player (e.g., Spotify) that provides playback control and metadata without a physical power state.

---

## Required Channels

These channels are mandatory for the functionality of the streaming service:

| **Channel** | **Description** | **Multiple** | **Details** |
|-------------|-----------------|--------------|-------------|
| `media_playback` | Playback controls and metadata for the streaming service. | No | [See details](/docs/system-architecture/supported-channels/media-playback-channel) |
| `device_information` | Service metadata, account/endpoint details, and operational statuses. | No | [See details](/docs/system-architecture/supported-channels/device-information-channel) |

---

## Use Case Scenarios

1. **Playback Control**:
    - Control music or video playback with play, pause, skip, and seek. View track/video information and album art.

2. **Queue Management**:
    - Navigate through playlists and manage playback queue.

3. **Multi-Device Playback**:
    - Control playback across different connected devices or speakers.

---

## Developer Notes

1. The media_playback channel is required for playback control.
2. No power control as these are cloud-based services.
3. Device information contains account and service details.
