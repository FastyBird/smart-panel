# Window Covering Device

<Callout type="info">
	**Device Category**: `window_covering`
</Callout>

The Window Covering device represents automated window treatments such as blinds, shades, or curtains.

---

## Required Channels

These channels are mandatory for the functionality of the window covering:

| **Channel** | **Description** | **Multiple** | **Details** |
|-------------|-----------------|--------------|-------------|
| `window_covering` | Controls coverings like blinds, curtains, and shutters, including position and tilt adjustments. | No | [See details](/docs/system-architecture/supported-channels/window-covering-channel) |
| `device_information` | Provides metadata about the device, including manufacturer details, model information, and operational statuses. | No | [See details](/docs/system-architecture/supported-channels/device-information-channel) |

---

## Optional Channels

These channels are optional and provide additional features:

| **Channel** | **Description** | **Multiple** | **Details** |
|-------------|-----------------|--------------|-------------|
| `battery` | Reports the status of a device's battery, including level, charging state, and operational health. | No | [See details](/docs/system-architecture/supported-channels/battery-channel) |
| `electrical_energy` | Measures the total energy consumption over time, typically reported in kilowatt-hours (kWh). | No | [See details](/docs/system-architecture/supported-channels/electrical-energy-channel) |
| `electrical_power` | Reports real-time power metrics such as voltage, current, and wattage for monitoring and diagnostics. | No | [See details](/docs/system-architecture/supported-channels/electrical-power-channel) |

---

## Use Case Scenarios

1. **Position Control**:
    - Open, close, or set specific positions for blinds or curtains. Support for tilt adjustment where applicable.

2. **Light Management**:
    - Adjust window coverings based on time of day or light levels for comfort and energy efficiency.

3. **Privacy Control**:
    - Quickly close all window coverings for privacy or security.

---

## Developer Notes

1. The window_covering channel is required for position control.
2. Battery channel is important for wireless motorized coverings.
3. Not all devices support tilt adjustment.
